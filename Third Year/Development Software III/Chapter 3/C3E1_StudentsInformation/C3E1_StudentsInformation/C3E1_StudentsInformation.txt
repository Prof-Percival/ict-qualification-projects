Students’ Information
Create an application that gathers student’s information from the following data:
• Student’s name
• Age
• Standard he/she is in
• Name of the school he/she attends

Assume the user has entered the following data:
Name: John Paul
Age: 12
Standard: 7
School: King’s College.

The application should have buttons that display the student’s information formatted in the following ways:
John Paul
John Paul is 12 years old
John Paul is in standard 7
John Paul goes to King’s College, School
John Paul, 12 years, standard 7
John Paul, 12 years, Standard 7, from King’s College
