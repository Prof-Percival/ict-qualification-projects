The National Weather Service’s uses the table below to describe the degree of cloudiness. 

The program must accept the percentage of cloud cover and display the appropriate descriptor. 

The program must do for 7 days.  

Use the following table:

	Percentage of Cloud Cover	Descriptor
		0 – 30		Clear
		31 – 70		Partly cloudy
		71 – 99		Cloudy
		100		overcast
